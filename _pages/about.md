Hi! Welcome to my website! I am Francisco, a researcher in Computational Mechanics. I work at Instituto Superior Técnico as an Invited Assistant Professor and I am a Researcher at IDMEC - Instituto de Engenharia Mecânica.

My work focus on advancing the predictive capabilities of numerical simulation tools, for the study of material and structural failure. In particular, my work has focused on multiphysics problems, such as electromechanical coupling in piezoelectric materials, multibody dynamics for fracture prediction in multibody systems and topology optimization for fracture resistant structural designs. To address these problems, I have developed different Peridynamic formulations, due to its inherent capabilities dealing with complex non-smooth problems. You can find more in the Research and Publications pages.

I have obtained my BSc, MSc and PhD degrees in Mechanical Engineering at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa.
