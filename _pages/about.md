# About

Hi! Welcome to my website! I am Francisco, a researcher in Computational Mechanics. I work at Instituto Superior Técnico as an Invited Assistant Professor and I am a Researcher at IDMEC - Instituto de Engenharia Mecânica.

My work focus on advancing the predictive capabilities of numerical simulation tools, for the study of material and structural failure. In particular, I have focused on multiphysics problems, such as in electromechanical coupling in piezoelectric materials, multibody dynamics for fracture prediction in multibody systems and topology optimization for fracture resistant structural designs. You can find more in the Research and Publications pages.

I have obtained my BSc, MSc and PhD degrees in Mechanical Engineering at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa.
